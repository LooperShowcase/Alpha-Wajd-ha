What Is Xbox?

Xbox is a video gaming brand that consists of five home video game consoles, as well as applications (games), streaming service Xbox Cloud Gaming,
and online services such as the Xbox network and Xbox Game Pass. The brand is produced by Microsoft Gaming,
a division of Microsoft. Xbox. Product type. Video gaming.

About The CEO Of Xbox Gaming

Phil Spencer is an American business executive and the CEO of Microsoft Gaming.
